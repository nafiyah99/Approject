Pick Your Room

To better serve students in dorm rooms, we provide cleaning services with a pricing model that adjusts based on room size. The base package sets the initial cost, with the final price reflecting your room's dimensions. Measure your room and select the appropriate size category for a fair and customized rate. We offer three distinct room size categories to help you find the best fit and pricing. Here are the room categories you can pick from for optimal pricing!

≤ 7 m²

Small

Perfect for students with cozy dorm rooms or compact living spaces. If your room is 7 square meters or less, this service is designed to keep your space tidy without breaking the bank. Ideal for those who value efficiency and minimalism.

7.1 - 11.5 m²

Regular

Ideal for students with a bit more space, such as a standard dorm room or a small studio apartment. This category covers rooms between 7.1 and 11.5 square meters, providing a balanced service that ensures thorough cleaning without unnecessary costs.

> 11.5 m²

Big

Suitable for students with larger living spaces, such as spacious studio apartments or shared living areas. For rooms over 11.5 square meters, our service ensures that every corner is spotless, offering a comprehensive cleaning solution that meets all your needs.
